St. Abigail's International

Welcome to
St. Abigail's International School

Our school is a faith based school. We run full British Curriculum with a blend of Nigerian books. Our teaching is 21st Century compliant, research based. We are vested in raising confident children who can take a stand tomorrow and make a change in our society. We apply a whole new approach to teaching, Employing deductive, analytical, decisive and critical method of teaching. Confidence impartation, symphony and musical adaptations, drama, writing, Exchange classrooms, art, field trips is at the core of our curriculum.
